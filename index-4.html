Daycare

Hours of Operation:

Monday - Friday 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM & 4:30 - 7:30 PM
Saturday - 9:00 AM - 12:00

Policies and Procedures:
  1. Babysitting is for memebers only.
  2. Members may not bring children other than their own.
  3. Parents must sign in on daycare sheet upon each visit.
  4. Parents must be on premises while children are in daycare.
  5. Daycare is available for children of ages 1 year to 12 years old.
  6. Please do not bring your children if they are sick.
  7. Parents will be called to change diapers or clothes when necessary.
  8. Children must stay in daycare area.
  9. Children are not allowed on or near equipment.
  10. Parents will be contacted to handle any disciplinary situation.
